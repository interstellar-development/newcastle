Sandyford

Closest Safe Area

Quiet, primarily residential neighbourhood sitting directly between Jesmond and the City Campus. Popular with students and professionals alike. Streets are calm, well-lit and leafy.

~8 min walk to uni
Crime: Low
Safe at night
  • Haymarket Metro just 5 min walk — connect citywide in minutes
  • Eldon Square bus interchange 6 min walk — dozens of routes

  • Short walk to Jesmond's Acorn Road & Brentwood Ave shopping
  • Close to Newcastle City Centre for major supermarkets & retail
  • Jesmond Dene park nearby for walks and outdoor relaxation
  • 2 bedroom flats from ~£950–£1,430/month
Jesmond
NE2 postcode
Safest / Most Prestigious

Newcastle's most sought-after residential area. Tree-lined streets, Victorian architecture, fantastic independent restaurants and boutiques. Consistently the lowest crime rate in the city.

~18 min walk to uni
Crime: Very Low
65 crimes/1,000
  • Jesmond Metro & West Jesmond Metro — direct line to city centre
  • Acorn Road: boutique shops, bakeries, wine bars, independent eateries
  • Brentwood Avenue: supermarkets, pharmacies, everyday essentials
  • Jesmond Dene — stunning riverside park on your doorstep
  • Regular bus routes directly to Northumbria University campus
  • Excellent selection of cafés for studying or leisure
  • 2 bedroom flats from ~£850–£1,590/month
Heaton
NE6 postcode
Best Value Safe Area

Vibrant, community-oriented neighbourhood with a lively café culture. Chillingham Road is the social hub — packed with independent bars, cafés and shops. Very popular with young professionals.

~20 min walk to uni
Crime: Low–Medium
Safe streets
  • Frequent buses along Chillingham Road direct to city centre & uni
  • Heaton Park — large green space perfect for daily walks
  • Chillingham Road: Sainsbury's, indie cafés, pubs, takeaways
  • Very affordable rents compared to Jesmond
  • Strong community feel — friendly neighbours and active local scene
  • Close to Byker Metro for quick connections across the network
  • 2 bedroom flats from ~£775–£1,250/month
City Centre
NE1 postcode
Most Convenient

Living in a secure, managed building in the city centre puts you at the heart of everything. Lively on weekends but well-policed. Choose a building with lobby security for peace of mind.

~10 min walk to uni
Busy nightlife area
Secured buildings
  • Monument, St James & Central Station Metro all within 10 min walk
  • Eldon Square — one of the UK's largest indoor shopping centres
  • Northumberland Street: major retail, M&S, Primark, John Lewis
  • Grainger Market: historic indoor market with fresh produce & cafés
  • Wide range of restaurants, gyms and cultural venues
  • Choose secured apartment buildings like The Forge or City Co Living
  • Serviced 2-bed flats from ~£1,500–£2,800/month (inc. bills)
